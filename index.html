Auto Tap

Auto tap for games like Tap Titans.

Tap with 3 fingers to activate in games/apps.
Tap with 2 fingers again to deactivate.

Note:
You can still tap around (with 1 finger) while Auto Tap is activated, but unable to drag / scroll.
